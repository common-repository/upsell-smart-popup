== Description ==
Introducing our revolutionary plugin that will make your shopping experience even better – the Upsell smart popup. This awesome plugin is built to work seamlessly with your Woocommerce store, giving you a series of popups that are strategically placed to get you the most bang for your buck at every step.

== Features and Detail ==
When you add a basic product to your cart, you'll see an irresistible upsell popup that shows you carefully selected additional products. It's a smart way to maximize the value of every purchase because you’re giving your customers irresistible options to take their shopping experience to the next level.

But that's not the end of the story. As your customers progress to the checkout page, another timely upsell pop-up greets them, offering them one last opportunity to maximize their order before they check out. This thoughtful placement makes sure that no upsell opportunity goes unnoticed.

Even on checkout, our plugin shines. A well-positioned upsell pop-up appears, offering one last tempting offer. This subtle nudge can make all the difference in your customer’s decision-making process, leading to a more positive shopping experience.

With our Upsell Popup Plugin, you can effortlessly boost sales, increase average order value, and provide a personalized shopping journey that keeps customers coming back for more.

== Installation ==
1: You need to go to WordPress admin area and visit Plugins » Add New page.
2: This will reveal the plugin upload form. Here you need to click on the 'Choose File' button and select the plugin file you downloaded earlier to your computer.
3: After you have selected the file, you need to click on the 'Install Now' button. WordPress will now upload the plugin file from your computer and
install it for you. You will see a success message like this after the installation is finished.
4: Once installed, you need to click on the Activate Plugin link to start using the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Q: What does the WooCommerce Popup Upsell Plugin do?
A: The Upsell Smart Popup Plugin is designed to display enticing pop-up upsell offers immediately after a customer adds a product to their cart. It helps increase your conversion rate and boost sales by encouraging customers to make additional purchases.

Q: How does the plugin work?
A: After a customer adds a product to their cart, the plugin triggers a pop-up window that displays a customized upsell offer. This offer can include related or complementary products, enticing discounts, and persuasive descriptions. The goal is to capture the customer's attention and encourage them to add more items to their order.

Q: Can I customize the upsell offers?
A: Absolutely! The plugin provides you with a range of customization options. You can choose the products you want to upsell.

Q: Is customer support available for the plugin?
A: Yes, we offer dedicated customer support for the WooCommerce Popup Upsell Plugin. If you encounter any issues or have questions, our support team is ready to assist you and ensure a smooth experience with the plugin.
